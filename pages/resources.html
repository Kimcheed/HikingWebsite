Resources for Hiking

Hiking presents a lovely oppurtunity for anyone to enjoy the beauty of nature and all of the its natural wonders. Whether you are an avid trekker or first time hiker, it is best to be prepared.

Top 7 Essential Resources for Hiking

  1. Proper Footwear

    One can opt for traditional hiking boots or hiking shoes depending on the distance and terrain of the trail. It is also important to know the difference between water repellent and water proof.

  2. Weather-Dependent Clothing

    Make sure you are aware of the weather conditions before you set out on the trail. If the weather is slightly cooler than usual, make sure you dress accordingly by wearing more layered clothing.

  3. First-Aid Kit

    Remember it is always better to be safe than sorry.

  4. Hiking Bag

    One way to keep your gear in check is to keep it all in a reliable hiking bag. Depending on the duration of the hike, perhaps a simple back pack may do.

  5. Trekking Poles or Walking Sticks

    Trekking are strongly advised for longer hikes, however; trekking Poles or walking sticks come in handy more than one could imagine. For example, they could be used to check leaves and brush in front of you in case of snakes. They are used to give additional support in times of uphill trails, downhill trails, or wet trails.

  6. Canteen to keep hyrdrated

    One of the most important items on the hike is water. Although some hikes may last no longer than an hour, it always important to stay hydrated.

  7. Dehydrated Food

Need to Know for Entry Level Hikers

  • Join a Hiking Club or Grab a Friend to accompany you on your first hike.

  • Research the trail or terrain before your first hike.

  • Check the weather forecast.

  • Make sure you bring proper equipment according to the hike.

  • Bring snacks.

  • Stay hydrated!
